Technology Behind HoloLux

HoloLux stands at the intersection of quantum mechanics and wearable technology. At its core, a Quantum Holographic Engine beams light through nano-reflectors to form 3D time projections that float mid-air, crisp and untethered.

The device features a full suite of motion-aware sensors and AI-assisted routines. Neural Gesture Recognition replaces physical buttons, enabling commands with the flick of a finger.
